About the Annapurna Circuit

The Annapurna Circuit is one of the most popular trekking routes in Nepal, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The trail passes through diverse terrains, including lush subtropical forests, terraced fields, and arid mountain landscapes.

Key Highlights

  • Thorong La Pass (5,416 meters) - A challenging yet rewarding experience.
  • Manang Village - Known for its stunning scenery and Tibetan culture.
  • Muktinath Temple - A sacred site for Hindus and Buddhists.

Whether you're an experienced trekker or a first-timer, the Annapurna Circuit promises an unforgettable adventure filled with natural beauty and cultural richness.
