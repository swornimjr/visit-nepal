Chitwan National Park

Discover Nepal's wildlife in one of the most famous parks in the world.

About Chitwan National Park

Chitwan National Park, located in the subtropical lowlands of Nepal, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to a rich variety of wildlife, including the famous one-horned rhinos and Bengal tigers.

Things to Do

  • Jungle Safari: Explore the forest and spot exotic animals.
  • Bird Watching: Watch hundreds of bird species, especially in the wetlands.
  • Canoe Rides: Paddle along the Rapti River and enjoy the scenic views.
  • Tharu Cultural Visit: Experience the culture of the local Tharu people.
